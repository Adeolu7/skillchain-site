The Mission

Traditional Professional Networks Are Broken.

Web2 career platforms are saturated with inflated resumes, unverified credentials, and slow, intermediary-gated payments. Builders waste time proving who they are instead of building what they want.


The Solution: Verification is On-Chain.

SkillChain leverages the Solana blockchain to create verifiable professional identities, trusted portfolios, secure peer-to-peer escrows, and direct connections between talent and builders.

The Talent OS

Verified On-Chain Profiles

Build a professional portfolio backed by blockchain verification. Showcase real skills, contributions, and achievements with absolute transparency.

Meet Your Peers

Connect with Web3 builders, developers, founders, creators, and professionals from across the Solana ecosystem.

Hire or Get Hired

Clients can post opportunities while freelancers discover gigs, projects, and long-term collaborations in one place.

Instant Payments

Receive payments directly in SOL and SKR without delays, banking fees, or traditional payment intermediaries.

Escrow-Backed Contracts

Secure gig agreements with milestone-based escrows. Funds are released programmatically upon verification of deliverable uploads.

Skill Verification Badges

Complete on-chain assessments and receive non-transferable verification NFTs to display on your digital resume.

P2P Tipping & Micropayments

Support developers or tip builders directly inside their professional feeds with instant, hardware-attested microtransactions.

Gated Workspace Hubs

Create or join exclusive developer and founder spaces gated by token holdings, specific NFTs, or verified skill levels.

AI-Powered Resume Match

Leverage our integrated AI agent to find matching developers, refine contract specs, or analyze portfolio relevance in seconds.
